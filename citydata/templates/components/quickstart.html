About GeoLink

Geolink allows you to create a global network and plan routes within it through air and road travel

To start, add some cities to your network in the Add Cities page.

Hub cities are the centers of your network, and are all connected to eachother by air

Other cities can be specified to have air access or not

Once you've added your cities, connect them in the Manage Connections page

You can dictate a set of parameters that define how your network is connected

The network is then automatically generated
